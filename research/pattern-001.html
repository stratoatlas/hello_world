What a pattern is

In StratoAtlas research, materials have three epistemic levels:

Observation "We saw this" — a single finding, not yet generalised
Case "Here is how it looked" — contextual evidence from a specific situation
Pattern "This repeats" — a falsifiable claim supported by multiple sources

A pattern is not a conclusion. It is a generalisable claim that can be challenged with a counter-case.
